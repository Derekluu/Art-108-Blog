Blog Post 7: JackBox

Playing Jackbox Games is a very fun, hilarious, and engaging experience! These games can be accessed and played from a phone or tablet when hosted through a computer. With the huge variety of games that involve either drawing or being able to input text, It provides a huge diverse assortment of entertainment. It encourages creativity and is a fun way for friends or classmates to bond together. The voting and collaboration for scoring in some games are very fun. The mechanics of the game are meant to pit people together to see who could come up with fun and creative ideas.

The audience feature makes the experience even more dynamic. Those not actively playing can still participate by voting on responses, shaping the outcomes in unexpected ways.
